# AskQanoon Functionalities

## Existing Features
Based on the current project capabilities:

1.  **Legal Information Retrieval (RAG)**
    *   **Description**: Users can ask natural language questions ("What is the punishment for theft?") and receive accurate answers sourced directly from Pakistan Penal Code (PPC), CrPC, and Family Laws.
    *   **Value**: Democratizes access to legal knowledge without needing to read complex statutes.

2.  **Bilingual Legal Assistance**
    *   **Description**: Users can ask questions in Roman Urdu ("FIR katwane ka tareeka kya hai?") and receive answers in the same easy-to-understand language.
    *   **Value**: Removes the language barrier for the majority of the Pakistani population.

3.  **Procedural Guidance**
    *   **Description**: Step-by-step guides for common legal processes (e.g., filing an FIR, cybercrime complaints, tenant eviction rules).
    *   **Value**: Empowers citizens to take correct legal action.

4.  **Source Verification**
    *   **Description**: Every answer includes citations (Law Name, Section Number) to allow users to verify the information independently.
    *   **Value**: Builds trust and ensures transparency.

## Proposed Features

### For General Users
1.  **Legal Document Generator (AI Drafts)**
    *   **Description**: Generate simple legal drafts like Rent Agreements, Affidavits, or Resignation Letters by answering a few simple questions.
    *   **Benefit**: Saves money on basic legal paperwork.

2.  **Inheritance Calculator (Islamic Law)**
    *   **Description**: Input family members and assets to calculate legal inheritance shares according to Pakistani/Islamic law.
    *   **Benefit**: Solves a complex, common problem instantly.

3.  **Voice-to-Text Legal Query**
    *   **Description**: Allow users to ask questions via voice note (Urdu/English) for better accessibility.
    *   **Benefit**: Critical for users with lower literacy or those uncomfortable typing.

### For Professionals & Community
4.  **Case Law Search (Precedents)**
    *   **Description**: Advanced search for previous Supreme Court / High Court judgments (Case Law) relevant to a specific query.
    *   **Benefit**: Invaluable tool for practicing lawyers.

5.  **Anonymous Legal Advice Forum**
    *   **Description**: A StackOverflow-style forum where users post scenarios anonymously, and verified lawyers provide general guidance.
    *   **Benefit**: Community building and user retention.

6.  **Know Your Rights (Daily Snippets)**
    *   **Description**: "Tip of the Day" notifications explaining a basic right (e.g., "Police cannot search your home without a warrant").
    *   **Benefit**: Educational and keeps the app top-of-mind.
